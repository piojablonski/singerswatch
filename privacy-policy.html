Singer's Watch Privacy Policy

The information we collect is used to improve the quality of our service, Singer's Watch application, and is not sold to or shared with others. Singer's Watch keeps the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us through email or any other communication details that enable us to contact you if you communicate with us by other means. We keep the information submitted through intentional activities such as account registration. When registering for Singer's Watch we ask for information such as email address and your name and password. Your password will be maintained on our system in an encrypted form.

For Singer's Watch Pro we keep your PayPal email and mobile phone number. We do not store or have access to sensitive billing information such as credit card or bank account details, even though you're paying for items via the app.

Singer's Watch is only available to users who are at least 13 years old. If you are under 13, please do not use our service.
